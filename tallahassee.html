State Capital

Welcome to the city of Tallahassee Fl, better know as the capitol of Florida. Located in the panhandle region of Florida betweens Gainesville and Jacksonville Florida. The city was founded in 1821 which makes it over 2 centuries old. Tallahasse is classified as an urban city and accoriding to the 2021 census report the population is around 197,102. The average income for indiviuals living in Tallahasse is around $38,000 anually where as Florida average income per capita is around $35,000 a year
